Announcing the Tufts   Education Program

 
New name, growing program.

It’s been 10 years since we launched as MSTE, before everyone said “STEM.” As much as we like our original name (and some of us think Math comes first!), we’ve decided it’s time to permute the letters.

We’re still all about discipline-based education research, with programs and projects involving faculty across Tufts STEM departments as well as the Center for Engineering Education and Outreach and the Center for Science and Mathematics Teaching.
 
In fact, we’ve just started an open rank search in Biology Education— a position in both Education and Biology.
